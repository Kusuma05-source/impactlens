How is this calculated?

Your Total CO₂ Saved is calculated by taking every activity logged by your community and multiplying it by our verified carbon conversion rates.

  • Waste Recycled: 0.75 kg CO₂ per kg
  • Sustainable Meals: 1.50 kg CO₂ per meal
  • Distance Biked: 0.21 kg CO₂ per km
  • Electricity Saved: 0.45 kg CO₂ per kWh
  • Water Saved: 0.002 kg CO₂ per Liter

Example: 100 kg of waste recycled × 0.75 = 75 kg of CO₂ offset!
